My 2 min recipe to make Maggi


MAGGI Noodles is always a constant companion. Enjoy Cheese MAGGI Noodles, Paneer MAGGI Noodles, Egg MAGGI Noodles, Veg MAGGI Noodles that add a twist of Flavour and yumminess to your everyday MAGGI Noodles.

Ingredients

  • MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
  • Olive oil
  • Capsicum
  • Chesse
  • Milk

Recipe Steps

  1. Prepare all the Ingredients
  2. Add water and boil maggi
  3. In a pan take some oil and heat it.Add capsicum
  4. Add the maggi to the pan and add chese and milk
  5. Cook for 2 mins and Serve hot
