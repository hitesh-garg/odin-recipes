Sriracha Honey Brussels Sprouts

Honey-Sriracha Brussels sprouts are healthy and full of flavor. They are roasted until crispy then coated with a delicious sweet and spicy sauce. A perfect side dish for any meal! Top with a drizzle of Sriracha sauce if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 Â½ teaspoons sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and cut in half

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a rimmed baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. Combine Brussels sprouts, olive oil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; toss until coated. Arrange in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until Brussels sprouts are golden brown and tender, about 25 minutes.
  4. Mix honey and Sriracha sauce together in a small bowl until combined.
  5. Place roasted Brussels sprouts in a bowl. Drizzle with honey mixture; toss until well coated.
